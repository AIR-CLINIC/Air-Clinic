Services

ONLINE MEDICAL CONSULTATIONS

This is the crux of our services. We have built a world class website where you can consult healthcare experts of different specialties globally for any type of ailment. Our service providers are on call 24/7 awaiting your call. Once they receive it, they consult with you and get you the best solutions for the situation.

Commonly used resources include;
  • Text Chat, Voice Chat, Video Chat, Graphs and Charts
  • Referral system: It will be easy for a medical practitioner to refer a client to another expert who either has more experience or is more specialized in a particular field.

OFFLINE MEDICAL CONSULTATIONS

Online services alone will not be enough to allow us to give that holistic approach in managing our patients. When we encounter cases that we can’t handle via the online means alone or in cases of emergency, we will have to use the offline method. In this scenario, we will be the “medical matchmakers” connecting health service experts and providers to clients who require these services in a speed of light manner. Think of us as the medical version of Uber or AirBnB. To allow us achieve this aim of “bringing hospitals to people”, we index health-experts, service providers, healthcare centers, emergency services on our server based on location. This will happen after verification of these services and individuals for quality through our criteria.

So when we get a got/beep, the available healthcare providers in the nearest location would be contacted to provide the service immediately. The following fields will be indexed. ;
  • INDEXING HEALTH EXPERTS- DOCTORS (Specialist and General Practitioner), Nurses, optometrists, physiotherapists, dieticians, pharmacists, medical laboratory scientists, etc
  • INDEXING HOSPITALS. Enough of long queues in hospitals because people go to hospitals. We are bringing hospitals to people. We choose the nearest available hospital that can provide a service based on the case at hand.
  • INDEXING LABORATORY SERVICES.
  • INDEXING PHARMACEUTICALS.
  • INDEXING EMERGENCY SERVICES. There is too much time wasted by emergency services in providing services because of its centralized nature. We will change that by decentralizing that sector so that the nearest ambulance to an emergency will be called.
